Selected Statistics
For the 2025 Baltimore City homicide list, I examined the age distribution of the victims.

Reasoning Behind My Pick
The distribution of ages can be used to determine whether certain demographic groups are disproportionately affected by violence. The histogram illustrates clustering patterns across age ranges and indicates whether homicides are concentrated among younger adults using 5-year bins.

Data Cleaning Notes
Regex was used to extract ages from victim entries. Rows with invalid ages were eliminated. 5-year bins were used to group the ages. The tabular histogram only prints non-empty bins.
